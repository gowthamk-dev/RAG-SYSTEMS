Scholarship Opportunities for 2nd Year Students:
1. Merit Scholarship: Available for students with CGPA > 9.0. Covers 50% of the tuition fee.
2. Sports Scholarship: For students who represented the college at national level.
3. Minority Scholarship: Eligibility includes family income below 2.5 LPA.
4. Application Process: Submit the scholarship application form at the administrative office by October 15th every year.
