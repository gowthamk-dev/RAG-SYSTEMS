Placement Policy:
1. Eligibility: Students must have zero active backlogs at the time of the recruitment process.
2. Minimum CGPA: Most companies require a minimum CGPA of 6.5 or above.
3. One Student One Job: Once a student is placed in a company, they are generally not allowed to sit for further drives, except for "Dream Company" opportunities.
4. Internship Approval: Internship requests must be approved by the Department Head and the Placement Cell.
