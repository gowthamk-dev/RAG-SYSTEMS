Attendance Policy:
1. Minimum Attendance: Every student must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each subject to be eligible to appear for the semester exams.
2. Medical Leave: Students with attendance between 65% and 75% may be allowed to sit for exams if they provide valid medical certificates.
3. Condonation Fee: A fee of 500 INR is charged for attendance condonation (only for those with 65-75% attendance).
4. Attendance below 65%: Students with attendance below 65% will be detained and must repeat the semester.
